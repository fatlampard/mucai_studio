朝著光的方向前進，是山的去向，也是大地的力量

山之光是盞山形吊燈，由二到四層堆疊的曲木環所組成。
溫暖的木頭搭配冰冷的水泥燈座呈現出強烈又溫和的對比。

The Light of The Mountain is a mountain shape pendant light. It's made of 2-4 different sized
bending plywood cone rings. The plywood cone rings with a concrete lamp holder presents the
warm and cold material contrasts.

山之光可以隨意轉動調整光線照明的方向，
可以照在牆上形成間接光源或照在任何需要光照的地方。

It's able to illuminate different direction by freely adjusting the position of those plywood cone rings.
You can illuminate the space or object you want to illuminate.

經過精確計算60度的斜角在製程上剛好可以讓木紋對齊，
整個曲木環看起來就像沒有接縫一樣。
在運送與收納時可以由小到大堆疊在一起減少使用空間。

The entire cone rings look like a seamless solid wooden piece
by accurately calculated the angle of the cone to make sure the seams are invisible.
It's able to stack together from the smallest to biggest to reduce the use of space during transport or storage.
