簡潔的三角結構

三重凳由三片從坐面延伸至椅腳的彎曲膠合板組成。
試圖在 2D 傳統曲木的限制下，彎曲出較多變化角度的椅凳。
中空的造型適合做為在桑拿房中使用的凳子，也適合大多數的環境。

The Triple of The Arch is constructed from three 10 mm bending birch plywoods extended from leg. It's tried to perform a multi-angle stool in the limit of 2D bending plywood process. And only the leg are joint together. The hollow triangle-shaped seating is perfect suit for sauna and also suit for most of situation.
